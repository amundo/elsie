About

This is a tool for transcribing audio in "real time."

If you type a phrase in the input and press enter, your text will be synced as a subtitle with the recording.

Pressing escape with toggle the player if you need more time to type.

The basic idea is to play the text, and then hit escape, and then type a bit of what you just heard. If you then rewind the audio, you should see your text approximately synced to the time when you typed.
